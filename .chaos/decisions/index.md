## How decisions flow here

1. A command hits a material choice → creates a runtime decision (Decision Center).
2. The answer is recorded as a **decision event** in the change's `decision-events.md`.
3. `chaos:sync` promotes durable decisions into `docs/decision-log/` or `docs/adr/`.
4. This index summarises the promoted decisions (area → source → posture → consequences).
