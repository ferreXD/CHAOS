The rules that make it safe

  • Risk = max-of signals — never averaged; unknown raises uncertainty, uncertainty raises the floor
  • Tie-break — ambiguous profile rounds UP at strict, DOWN otherwise (escalation is cheap)
  • Escalation self-applies — at task boundaries, evidence reused by hash; approval decision only if a new gate demands one
  • Downgrades need a human — mechanically: history entries moving an axis down require a human decisionRef; doctor flags violations as blockers
  • n/a ≠ skipped — verify records satisfied / waived / not-applicable-with-reason per gate, audited against signals
  • Visible restraint — every skipped ceremony is listed with why it's safe — the trust mechanism
  • Calibration is governed — retro proposes rule-table changes as normal proposals; thresholds never drift silently; repo-scoped, never per-user
